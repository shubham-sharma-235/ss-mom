About SS-MOM 2027

Smart & Sustainable Manufacturing Research

SSMOM 2027 is an international platform for researchers, PhD scholars, postdoctoral fellows, professors, scientists, and industry experts to share cutting-edge research in smart, digital, and sustainable industrial systems. The conference focuses on emerging technologies including AI/ML, digital twins, cyber-physical systems, IoT, Industry 4.0/5.0, and sustainable manufacturing, applied to materials, manufacturing, and operations.

The conference promotes interdisciplinary collaboration and targets sustainable development goals (SDGs) by exploring energy-efficient, environmentally responsible, and resource-optimized engineering solutions. SSMOM 2027 brings together global researchers on a single platform to exchange knowledge, foster innovation, and build lasting academic and industry networks.

We cordially invite Academicians, Researchers, Technocrats, and Students to share their findings and experiences in the field of smart and sustainable manufacturing, operations, and materials on behalf of the Department of Mechanical Engineering, Manipal University Jaipur, India.

About Manipal University Jaipur

The Manipal Education and Medical Group is an established leader in the field of education, research and healthcare. In a span of over seven decades, it has transformed the lives of more than 3,00,000 students from over 59 countries. The group includes five universities across India, Caribbean Islands, and Malaysia.

Manipal University Jaipur (MUJ) was launched in 2011 on an invitation from the Government of Rajasthan. Situated on 122 Acres at Dehmi Kalan village near Jaipur, MUJ offers career-oriented multi-disciplinary programmes at UG, PG, and doctoral levels.

D

About the Department of Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical Engineering is a diverse and dynamic field encompassing the design of intricate components to the development of large-scale systems. The B.Tech programme equips students with skills to design and build the machines of the future, with access to state-of-the-art laboratories and a well-stocked library.

In addition to undergraduate studies, the department offers a rigorous Ph.D. programme that fosters advanced research in core and interdisciplinary areas of Mechanical Engineering, guided by experienced faculty and supported by well-equipped research labs. The department also covers emerging fields like medical engineering and defence technology.
