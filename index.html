About the Conference

A Premier International
Research Platform

SSMOM 2027 brings together researchers, PhD scholars, postdoctoral fellows, professors, scientists, and industry experts to share cutting-edge research in smart, digital, and sustainable industrial systems — with focus on AI/ML, digital twins, cyber-physical systems, IoT, and Industry 4.0/5.0.

Manipal University Jaipur

Part of the globally recognised Manipal Education and Medical Group, MUJ was launched in 2011 and has transformed the lives of 3,00,000+ students from 59 countries. Set on a 122-acre campus near Jaipur, it offers multi-disciplinary programmes at UG, PG, and doctoral levels with state-of-the-art research facilities.

Department of Mechanical Engineering

A dynamic and diverse academic unit spanning aerospace, automotive, energy, defence, and emerging engineering fields. Offering B.Tech and a rigorous Ph.D. programme, the department is supported by well-equipped laboratories and faculty engaged in cutting-edge interdisciplinary research.

Research Tracks

Three Interdisciplinary
Research Tracks

All topics relating to cutting-edge research in manufacturing, operations, and materials are welcome. The conference promotes interdisciplinary collaboration and targets the UN SDGs through energy-efficient and resource-optimised engineering solutions.

01
Smart & Sustainable Manufacturing
Intelligent, digital, and sustainable manufacturing leveraging AI, CPS, and Industry 4.0/5.0
  • 3D Printing / Additive Manufacturing
  • AI/ML-Driven Process Optimization
  • Digital Twin-Based Process Modeling
  • Robotics, Automation & Collaborative Robots
  • IoT-Enabled Smart Factories
  • Cyber-Physical Systems for Manufacturing
  • Predictive Quality Control & Maintenance
  • Green & Sustainable Manufacturing Techniques
  • Micro and Nano Machining
  • AR/VR-Assisted Manufacturing
  • Precision & Ultra-Precision Processes
  • Hybrid Manufacturing Processes
02
Smart & Sustainable Operations & Industrial Engineering
Data-driven, smart, and sustainable industrial operations, supply chain, and decision-making systems
  • Digital Twin-Driven Industrial Operations
  • AI/ML-Enabled Supply Chain Optimization
  • Lean, Agile & Resilient Operations
  • Energy-Efficient Industrial Systems
  • Circular Economy & Resource Recovery
  • Big Data Analytics & Process Mining
  • Smart Factory Modeling & Simulation
  • Operations Intelligence for Industry 4.0/5.0
  • Risk Assessment & Mitigation Strategies
  • Production Scheduling & Logistics
  • Human-in-the-Loop Cyber-Physical Systems
  • Sustainability Assessment & Environmental Modeling
03
Smart & Sustainable Materials
Development, characterization, and applications of multifunctional, intelligent, and sustainable materials
  • Smart Materials (Shape-Memory, Piezoelectric)
  • Nanomaterials & Nanocomposites
  • AI/ML-Driven Materials Design
  • Digital Twin-Based Materials Modeling
  • Sustainable & Biodegradable Materials
  • Additive Manufacturing of Novel Materials
  • Biomimetic & Bio-Inspired Materials
  • Advanced Characterization (XRD, SEM, TEM, AFM)
  • Life-Cycle Assessment & Circular Economy
  • 2D Materials & Metamaterials
  • Computational Materials Science
  • High-Throughput Experimentation

Peer Review

A Rigorous
Review Process

Each submitted manuscript receives a unique paper ID and undergoes a multi-round evaluation by our technical programme committee.

1

Submission

Paper receives a unique ID. Initial screening checks for conference scope and originality.

2

Technical Review

Manuscript sent to domain experts for thorough technical peer review.

3

Revision

Author notified of outcome. Revised manuscript to be submitted within 15 days if required.

4

Final Evaluation

Third-round evaluation by the Technical Programme Committee for final decision.

5

Publication

Accepted papers published in SCOPUS-indexed proceedings and international journals.
