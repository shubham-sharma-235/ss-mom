About SS-MOM 2027

SSMOM 2027 is an international platform for researchers, PhD scholars, postdoctoral fellows, professors, scientists, and industry experts to share cutting-edge research in smart, digital, and sustainable industrial systems. The conference focuses on emerging technologies including AI/ML, digital twins, cyber-physical systems, IoT, Industry 4.0/5.0, and sustainable manufacturing applied to materials, manufacturing, and operations.

The conference promotes interdisciplinary collaboration and targets sustainable development goals (SDGs) by exploring energy-efficient, environmentally responsible, and resource-optimized engineering solutions. We cordially invite academicians, researchers, technocrats, and students to share their findings on behalf of the Department of Mechanical Engineering, Manipal University Jaipur, India.

U

About Manipal University Jaipur

The Manipal Education and Medical Group has transformed the lives of 3,00,000+ students from 59 countries across five universities. MUJ was launched in 2011 on an invitation from the Government of Rajasthan.

Situated on 122 Acres near Jaipur, MUJ offers career-oriented programmes at UG, PG, and doctoral levels with state-of-the-art research facilities and a modern digital library.

D

Department of Mechanical Engineering

A diverse and dynamic academic unit spanning aerospace, automotive, energy, defence, and emerging engineering domains. The B.Tech programme equips students with skills to design and build the machines of the future, backed by well-equipped laboratories and expert faculty.

The department also offers a rigorous Ph.D. programme in core and interdisciplinary areas of Mechanical Engineering — guided by experienced researchers and supported by advanced research labs.
